How it works ?

SaversBasket works on the principle of crowdsourcing. Remember We built SaversBasket to solve one of our own problems - and we knew from the start it would require the efforts and time of like-minded people. Much as we take things on our own to provide the best service. So we built this tool in such a way, to enable to harness the collective power of people.

This is where our service steps in. Now visualize, all the people sharing their piece of information after each grocery shopping and we format that information to provide greater value to those who are looking for up-to-date prices. This is the collective power of the people. In another word, we are crowdsourcing the task of providing grocery prices via you and a greater super-set of people.

We are using the same principle to power the SaversBasket system. Only thing changed is, we're are harnessing different prices input by different people and presenting in a relevant and useful format !
